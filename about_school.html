WELCOME TO GREEN VALLEY PUBLIC SCHOOL MADHUBANI

Green Valley Public School Madhubani is located at Ranti Deorhi Madhubani. The location offers a perfect mix of serenity and convenience. It has a sprawling campus in tranquil surroundings, set against the backdrop of lush green fields and trees. Green Valley Public School Madhubani is a C.B.S.E. based, Co-ed English medium school, designed to provide world class education along with best facilities in transportation, sports, student care, etc. It boasts of excellent faculty and an advanced curriculum developed by experts to prepare students in the best possible way for their life ahead. The learning methodology is designed to be student friendly, and extends beyond mere classrooms and class hours.
